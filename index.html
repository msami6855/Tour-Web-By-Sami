The Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France, is an iconic symbol of both the city and the country. This wrought-iron lattice tower stands as a testament to French engineering and artistry. 02. Taj Mahal Tour

The Taj Mahal, situated in Agra, India, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most recognized and admired architectural wonders in the world. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, it is a stunning white marble mausoleum renowned for its intricate craftsmanship and symmetrical beauty.

03. Petronas Twin Towers Tour

The Petronas Twin Towers, located in the capital city Kuala Lumpur, are among the most iconic landmarks in Malaysia. These twin skyscrapers were the tallest buildings in the world when they were completed in 1998. The towers are connected by a skybridge, providing breathtaking views of the city. The modern and sleek architecture of the Petronas Twin Towers reflects Malaysia's progress and development while serving as a symbol of national pride.

05. Great Wall of China Tour

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built across historical northern borders of China to protect against invasions. While it spans multiple cities and regions in China, its most famous sections include Badaling and Mutianyu. The wall showcases the ancient engineering prowess of China and offers spectacular views of the surrounding landscapes.
